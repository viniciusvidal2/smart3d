Update camera...

This menu item is used to adjust the camera posture and focal distance.

Usage

  1. Select a camera in the mesh tree.





  2. Use the Menu "File > Update camera..." to activate the tool.
  3. In the lower left corner will be displayed the step used to in the operations and the mode of operation (move camera or pitch/yaw).





  4. Use the keys to update the camera parameters:

    • W - Increase pitch/move camera up
    • A - Increase yaw/move camera left
    • S - Decrease pitch/move camera down
    • D - Decrease yaw/move camera right

    • Q - Increase focal distance
    • E - Decrease focal distance

    • R - Increase the step used to move the camera and change pitch/yaw
    • F - Decrease the step used to move the camera and change pitch/yaw

    • T - Change between move the camera and pitch/yaw

  5. Use the Menu "File > Update camera..." to deactivate the tool.
